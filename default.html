Welcome

Welcome to SkylarHiebert.com. Here you can find my resume, a list of programming projects, and contact information for Skylar Hiebert. Over the past two years Skylar has been developing applications for various in class projects. In addition, he participated in the Google Summer of Code for 2011 working with Portland State University on the FreeQuiz project.

Activities

Skylar is currently presiding as Chair to the IEEE Student Branch at Washington State University Vancouver. He has been working hard to re-build this student branch since October of 2010. Successful events include speakers from Intel and Hewlett-Packard and an end-of-year celebration. Skylar is currently in the midst of arranging a technical career fair to be put on at the university.
